The Science

Why Choose
Lab Grown?

01
Chemically Identical

Lab grown sapphires are corundum (Al₂O₃) — exactly the same chemical composition as mined sapphires. A gemologist cannot tell the difference without specialist equipment.

02
Ethically Sourced

Zero mining, zero displacement, zero environmental damage. Created in controlled laboratory conditions with a fraction of the ecological footprint.

03
Exceptional Value

Get a stunning, certified stone at a fraction of the cost of a mined equivalent — allowing you to choose a larger, finer gem for your budget.

04
All Colours Available

From deep royal blue to padparadscha pink-orange, vivid yellow, and pure white — any hue you desire, consistent and vibrant every time.

Our Stones

The Collection

Every stone set in custom jewellery, certified, and ready to ship or collect in Galle.

Royal Blue Sapphire
Classic Ceylon Blue

The iconic deep blue that made Ceylon famous — rich, velvety, and electric. Available in rounds, ovals, cushions, and emerald cuts.

Pink Sapphire
Blush to Hot Pink

From delicate blush to vivid hot pink — romantic, feminine, and enduringly popular for engagement rings and pendants.

Yellow Sapphire
Golden to Canary

Warm and luminous, from soft champagne to intense canary yellow. A stunning alternative to yellow diamond.

Padparadscha
Lotus Pink-Orange

The rarest and most prized sapphire variety — a delicate blend of pink and orange inspired by the lotus flower. Extraordinary.

White Sapphire
Pure & Brilliant

Colourless and brilliant — an ethical, durable alternative to diamond for centre stones, halos, and pavé settings.

Green Sapphire
Forest to Teal

From earthy forest green to bright teal, green sapphires offer a distinctive, contemporary alternative to emerald.
